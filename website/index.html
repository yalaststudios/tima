Quick start

First 10 minutes with Tima

  1. Add your weekly classes first so the app can build a useful routine around them.
  2. Open the Events page and add major deadlines before they become urgent.
  3. Turn on reminders and relax battery restrictions so alerts arrive on time.
  4. Export a backup once your setup feels right.
